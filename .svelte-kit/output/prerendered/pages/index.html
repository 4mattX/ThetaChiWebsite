About Us

Theta Chi is one of the oldest men’s college fraternities in North America. Founded in 1856, Theta Chi Fraternity is a men’s collegiate fraternity with more than 200,000 initiated members and has established 245 chapters.

Theta Rho is among these 245 chapters. We live by our motto "An Assisting Hand" through our philanthropy endeavors and inclusivity.
